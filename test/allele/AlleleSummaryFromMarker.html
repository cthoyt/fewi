  2. For a marker, from the hyperlinked count for alleles associated with disease models (OMIM annotations), the report includes all alleles of all types from any genotypes annotated to disease models EXCLUDING only "Not" annotations. Listings in the "Human Disease Models" column DISPLAY ONLY disease terms where the allele participates in an OMG or "Simple" genotype, and SUPPRESS disease terms where the allele participates in a "Complex" genotype.
