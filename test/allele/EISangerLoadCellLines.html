EI Genotype module: loading mutant cell lines

  • User story 55, Modify Sanger loader to add cell lines

Acceptance Criteria

  • Sanger cell line data are loaded correctly into MGI's database
  • incorrect data are rejected and error reports are sent to appropriate staff
  • new genotypes are created where needed to accommodate unique cell lines
  • MP data are loaded and associated with the correct (possibly new) genotypes

See also: PhenotypeAnnotLoader for tests of the load script.

Tests

Skip to Manual Tests | Automated Tests

Summary of Tests:

  • Confirm cell lines get associated with 2 alleles in homozygous genotypes, only Allele1 in heterozygous, hemizygous, or indeterminate genotypes
  • Confirm cell lines get associated with their correct alleles
  • Confirm new genotypes get created for same allele, same allele state but different cell line
  • Confirm MP annotations get associated with genotype having the correct cell line(s)
  • Confirm alleles not part of the Sanger load don't have cell lines associated yet
