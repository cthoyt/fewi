Cre User-Submitted Notes Tests

  • User story 155, Cre User-submitted notes

Acceptance Criteria

  • Cre curator has a mechanism for entering usage/experience notes (from users, the literature, or wherever) associated with a specific Cre allele
  • The WI displays the notes in appropriate places. Also makes clear that user submitted notes are welcome
  • Users can communicate notes about Cre usage/experiences through several mechanisms (email, YourInputWelcome, phone, etc). Whoever receives the info (e.g., user support) forwards Cre notes to the Cre editor, who may then enter the note.

Tests

Marked tests for regression with high or low.
