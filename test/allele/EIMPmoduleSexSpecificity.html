EI MP module testing of Sex Specificity

  • User story 7, Modify EI for Sex Specificity

Acceptance Criteria

  • Add a column named "sex" to the annotation section of the MP module
  • Add ability to enter new annotations with sex data included
  • Add ability to modify sex data on existing annotations
  • Add ability to delete sex data from exiting annotations
  • Add ability to search for annotations by sex data

Tests

Skip to Manual Tests | Automated Tests

Manual tests

  1. Verify Sex column exists

    jeffc 8/28/2012

    1. Open the Scrum-dog EI
    2. Log in to the Scrum-dog EI
    3. Click on the MP Annotations module button
    4. Verify there is a column called Sex between the EVI and Modified By columns
  1. Verify you can enter new annotations with sex data included

    jeffc 8/28/2012

    1. Open the Scrum-dog EI
    2. Log in to the Scrum-dog EI
    3. Click on the MP Annotations module button
    4. Enter MGI:2654760 in the MGI Accession ID field and click Search
    5. In the first empty row of the Annotation section enter: Term Acc ID: MP:0009790, J: 13466, Evi: EXP, Sex: M
    6. In the second empty row of the Annotation section enter: Term Acc ID: MP:0001105, J: 13466, Evi: EXP, Sex: F
    7. Click on the Save Annotations button
    8. Verify both terms have been saved
  1. Verify you can modify sex data of an annotation

    jeffc 8/28/2012

    1. Open the Scrum-dog EI
    2. Log in to the Scrum-dog EI
    3. Click on the MP Annotations module button
    4. Enter MGI:2654844 in the MGI Accession ID field and click Search
    5. In the row for MP:0011085 change the Sex field from NA to M
    6. Click on the Save Annotations button
    7. Verify the sex has been saved
    8. In the row for MP:0000745 change the Sex field from NA to F
    9. Click on the Save Annotations button
    10. Verify the sex has been saved
  1. Verify you can not delete sex data of an annotation

    jeffc 8/28/2012

    1. Open the Scrum-dog EI
    2. Log in to the Scrum-dog EI
    3. Click on the MP Annotations module button
    4. Enter MGI:2654844 in the MGI Accession ID field and click Search
    5. In the row for MP:0002064 change the Sex field from NA to empty
    6. Click on the Save Annotations button
    7. Verify the sex has not been changed
  1. Verify you can search sex data of an annotation

    jeffc 8/28/2012

    1. Open the Scrum-dog EI
    2. Log in to the Scrum-dog EI
    3. Click on the MP Annotations module button
    4. Enter M in the Sex field and click Search
    5. Verify the results returned include MGI:2654760 and MGI:2654844
