Cre Summary Tests

Acceptance Criteria

  • US118 - The Detected in and Not detected in lists now each have their own column
  • US118 - When no data is available for Detected in or Not Detected in, leave the field blank
  • US118 - Sorting works for all sortable columns
  • US177 - Querying by a subsystem term also highlights the correct systems involved
  • US177 - Querying by a synonym term highlights the correct systems involved
  • US177 - Querying by an ambiguous term highlights all matching systems
  • US177 - Querying by an EMAP term highlights all matching systems
  • US177 - Querying by an EMAP synonym term highlights all matching systems
  • US177 - Querying by a term highlights all matching systems for both Detected and Not Detected columns
  • Project Blizzard - US49 - Display inducer label on Summary(not just yes/no)
