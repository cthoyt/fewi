Tabs Helper Text

  • User story 92, Help Text on Tabs

Acceptance Criteria

  • Helping text will display on the grid tab when searching for a disease or phenotype
  • Helping text will display on the grid tab when searching for a gene
  • Helping text will display on the grid tab when searching for a disease or phenotype and a gene
  • Helping text will display on the genes tab when searching for a disease or phenotype
  • Helping text will display on the genes tab when searching for a gene
  • Helping text will display on the genes tab when searching for a disease or phenotype and a gene
  • Helping text will display on the diseases tab when searching for a disease or phenotype
  • Helping text will display on the diseases tab when searching for a gene
  • Helping text will display on the diseases tab when searching for a disease or phenotype and a gene
